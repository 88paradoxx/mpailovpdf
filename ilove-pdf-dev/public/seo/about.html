About ilovpdf

Image Tools • PDF Tools • Privacy • Contact

Local-First Editing

ilovpdf is built around a local-first architecture where image and PDF processing happens directly in the browser instead of on remote servers.

Built for Professionals

The tools are tuned for designers, developers, marketers, and teams who need fast, precise edits without compromising on privacy or control.

Key Principles

  • Client-side processing for documents and images
  • No mandatory sign-ups or account creation
  • Modern browser-based UI with single-page navigation

FAQ

Is ilovpdf an official desktop application?
No. ilovpdf runs in the browser and does not install native software on your machine.
Do you store my documents?
The tools are designed so that processing is handled locally and documents do not leave your browser.
Who is ilovpdf for?
Anyone who needs a fast, privacy-focused editor for PDFs and images, from individual creators to internal teams.
