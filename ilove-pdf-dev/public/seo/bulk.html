FAQ

How many images can I process at once?
The tool is designed for up to around 100 images per batch, depending on browser limits.
Do I need to zip files before upload?
No. Select multiple files and download results as a ZIP archive.
Are images transferred to a server?
No. Processing happens entirely on your machine.
