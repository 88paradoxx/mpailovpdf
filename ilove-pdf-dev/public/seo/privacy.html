Privacy Policy

Local Processing

Image and document operations are executed inside your browser using local compute. Files are not uploaded to remote servers for processing.

Minimal Analytics

Usage metrics, if collected, are aggregated to improve performance and stability and are not tied to document contents.

Data Controls

You maintain control of your files at all times. Closing the browser tab clears in-memory processing data.

FAQ

Do you upload PDFs or images to your servers?
The tools are designed to work with local browser memory so that documents do not need to be uploaded for editing.
Do you share data with third parties?
Any analytics or operational telemetry is used to maintain and improve the service and is not sold to third parties.
Can I use ilovpdf without an account?
Yes. Most tools do not require authentication and can be used anonymously.
